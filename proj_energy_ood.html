Robust OOD Detection via Energy

Deploys energy-based modeling to separate in-distribution vs. unknowns without brittle heuristics. The design hardens inference against shifting data and adversarial drift.

  • Energy fine-tuning with margin constraints and temperature scaling
  • Stress-tested under covariate & semantic shift; adversarial probes
  • Plug-and-play for ResNet/ViT backbones; zero change to classifiers
