Uncertainty Quantification

Description: This work focuses on quantifying predictive uncertainty in deep learning systems to improve model reliability and decision-making under distributional shift. It explores principled ways to distinguish between model confidence and true epistemic uncertainty, emphasizing calibration, robustness, and interpretability.
