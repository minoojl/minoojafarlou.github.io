Advancing Robust Out-of-Distribution Detection Across Modalities

Research-in-progress · Multi-modal (Vision + NLP)

This body of work develops next-generation frameworks for dependable out-of-distribution (OOD) detection in modern AI systems. The agenda centers on strengthening robustness, uncertainty awareness, and generalization under shifting data conditions—without disclosing implementation specifics. The studies span complementary paradigms and architectures, with active extensions across both vision and language, targeting scalable, modality-agnostic OOD detection for open-world deployment.
