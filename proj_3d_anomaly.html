3D Anomaly Detection

This project explores volumetric convolutional neural networks for detecting structural anomalies in 3D data (e.g., CT scans, industrial parts). The emphasis is on learning sparse latent representations that are robust to noise and misalignment.

Technical Details

The pipeline integrates voxelized feature extraction, energy-based scoring, and uncertainty estimation via MC-dropout. Training uses a hybrid loss with reconstruction and margin penalties to separate inlier/outlier distributions.

Future Work

Extending the framework to multi-modal fusion (CT + MRI) and incorporating weak supervision from domain experts.
