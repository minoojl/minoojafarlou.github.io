Research Experience

Researcher, Department of Computer Science, Georgia State University 2023 – Present
Conducting research in trustworthy multimodal AI: calibrated uncertainty, OOD/anomaly detection, robust training.

Research Assistant, TReNDS Center (GSU / Georgia Tech / Emory) 2022 – 2024
Contributed to multimodal causality research integrating MEG and fMRI neuroimaging with clinical variables for brain connectivity and disorder analysis, including preprocessing and quality control of large-scale neuroimaging datasets.

Conducted research on GPS trajectory mining and spatiotemporal data analysis; developed a clustering framework using DBSCAN to identify mobility patterns and anomalies in large-scale moving object databases, resulting in a peer-reviewed publication.

Graduate Research Assistant, Computer Vision Lab, Kharazmi University 2014 – 2016
Explored deep learning methods for optical character recognition and image captioning, focusing on visual feature extraction, sequence modeling, and cross-modal alignment between image and text representations.
